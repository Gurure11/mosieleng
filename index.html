About Mosieleng Technical College

Your Gateway to Practical Skills and Quality Education. Mosieleng Technical College is a premier institution dedicated to providing high-quality education and practical skills to empower students for the modern workforce. Our college offers a diverse range of programs designed to equip learners with industry-relevant knowledge and hands-on experience in their chosen fields.

Our Courses

Engineering Studies (N1 - N6)

The Engineering Studies Department offers comprehensive training programs designed to equip students with technical knowledge and hands-on skills in various engineering fields. These courses provide a pathway to employment, apprenticeships, or further studies in engineering disciplines.

Apply Now

Electrical Engineering (N1 - N6)

  • Learn electrical principles, circuits, and power systems.
  • Gain hands-on experience in wiring, fault-finding, and electrical installations.
  • Understand electrical maintenance, safety regulations, and industrial applications.
  • Prepares students for careers as electricians, electrical technicians, or further engineering studies.

Civil Engineering (N1 - N6)

  • Study construction methods, structural design, and material sciences.
  • Learn surveying, site planning, and project management principles.
  • Understand water supply systems, roads, and building infrastructure.
  • Suitable for careers in construction, urban development, and civil project management.

Mechanical Engineering (N1 - N6)

  • Gain knowledge in machine design, thermodynamics, and fluid mechanics.
  • Learn about industrial machinery, maintenance, and automation systems.
  • Develop skills in mechanical drafting and technical drawing.
  • Prepares students for roles in manufacturing, engineering workshops, and industrial maintenance.

Motor & Diesel Mechanics (N1 - N6)

  • Learn the fundamentals of motor vehicle and diesel engine mechanics.
  • Develop diagnostic, repair, and maintenance skills for vehicles and heavy machinery.
  • Understand fuel injection systems, engine performance, and vehicle electronics.
  • Career opportunities in automotive repair workshops, fleet maintenance, and transport industries.

Plumbing (N1 - N6)

  • Learn plumbing installation, maintenance, and drainage systems.
  • Gain hands-on experience in pipefitting, water supply, and sanitation systems.
  • Understand plumbing regulations, blueprint reading, and safety protocols.
  • Ideal for careers in residential, commercial, and industrial plumbing.

Fitting & Turning (N1 - N6)

  • Learn precision machining, metal cutting, and shaping techniques.
  • Gain hands-on experience with lathes, milling machines, and grinding tools.
  • Understand blueprint reading, mechanical fitting, and manufacturing processes.
  • Prepares students for careers in engineering workshops, toolmaking, and production industries.

Computer Skills Course

The Computer Skills Course department offers specialized training programs designed to equip students with essential digital and administrative skills for various professional roles. These courses focus on practical computer literacy, office administration, and customer service to ensure graduates are workplace-ready.

Apply Now

Computer Clerk

  • Learn fundamental and advanced computer operations.
  • Develop data entry, file management, and document processing skills.
  • Gain proficiency in office software such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Understand digital record-keeping and administrative procedures.

Call Centre Operations

  • Master effective telephone etiquette and customer service communication.
  • Learn call-handling techniques, including inbound and outbound call management.
  • Understand customer relationship management (CRM) systems.
  • Develop problem-solving and conflict-resolution skills for client interactions.

Office Assistant

  • Acquire clerical and administrative support skills.
  • Learn how to manage office correspondence, scheduling, and filing systems.
  • Develop organizational skills to assist with business operations.
  • Gain experience in basic bookkeeping and office workflow management.

Receptionist & Secretary

  • Master front-desk operations and visitor management.
  • Learn professional communication and business correspondence.
  • Develop skills in appointment scheduling and office coordination.
  • Gain experience in handling confidential documents and executive support.

Receptionist & Stock Controller

  • Combine receptionist duties with inventory management skills.
  • Learn stock control, tracking, and inventory documentation.
  • Develop customer service skills for front-desk and supplier interactions.
  • Understand the use of stock management software for business operations.

Sports Management

The Sports Management Department provides students with the skills needed to plan, coordinate, and manage sporting events, corporate functions, and entertainment activities. These courses prepare graduates for dynamic careers in the sports industry, event planning, and hospitality sectors.

Apply Now

CATHSSETA Sports Events Management (Level 5)

  • Learn event planning, budgeting, and logistical coordination for sports events.
  • Gain expertise in sponsorship management, marketing, and media relations.
  • Understand sports facility management, athlete coordination, and event safety regulations.
  • Prepares students for roles in sports federations, event companies, and recreational organizations.

Events Management (Level 4)

  • Develop skills in organizing corporate events, weddings, conferences, and exhibitions.
  • Learn how to manage event logistics, vendor coordination, and guest services.
  • Gain knowledge in event budgeting, marketing, and customer experience management.
  • Ideal for careers in hospitality, event planning agencies, and entertainment industries.

Sports Management (Level 4 - Level 5)

  • Learn the business aspects of sports organizations, team management, and player development.
  • Gain knowledge in sports marketing, sponsorships, and public relations.
  • Understand facility operations, league administration, and athlete performance analysis.
  • Suitable for careers in professional sports teams, fitness centers, and sports academies.

N3 Technical Matric

The Academic Programs Department is designed to help students achieve their educational goals by providing essential learning support, matric completion opportunities, and revision classes. These programs are ideal for students looking to improve their academic performance or obtain their matric certification.

Apply Now

Matric Re-write (Grade 12)

  • Tailored for students who want to improve their matric results and increase their career and study opportunities.

Revision Classes (Grade 8 - 12)

  • Ideal for students who want to improve their grades and build a strong academic foundation.

Business Studies (N4 - N6)

The Business & Management Studies Department provides students with essential administrative, managerial, and communication skills needed for various business environments. These programs equip graduates with both theoretical knowledge and practical expertise, preparing them for careers in corporate, public, and educational sectors.

Apply Now

Management Assistant (N4 - N6)

Public Relations (N4 - N6)
